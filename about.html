Hi, I'm Omar Alali. I'm from Saudi Arabia and a Cybersecurity graduate from Florida International University (FIU) with a 🏆 3.97 / 4.00 GPA. I also earned an Undergraduate Certificate in Ethics, Big Data, and Artificial Intelligence.

My journey into cybersecurity started unexpectedly. While studying Business in 2018, I wanted a gaming PC but couldn't afford a prebuilt one. Instead, I bought the parts and taught myself how to build it by watching YouTube videos and reading manuals. Successfully building that computer sparked my passion for technology and completely changed my career goals.

At the time, I didn't speak English, so I spent a year learning English before moving to the United States to study cybersecurity.

Today, I'm passionate about 🔐 cybersecurity, ☁️ cloud computing, 🧠 artificial intelligence, 🌐 networking, automation, and emerging technologies. I enjoy building hands-on projects, continuously learning new skills, and earning certifications while exploring different areas of technology and security.

My goal is to grow into a well-rounded technology and security professional — as a Cloud Security Engineer, Security Engineer, or SOC Analyst — while continuing to explore AI, automation, and other emerging areas of technology and security.
